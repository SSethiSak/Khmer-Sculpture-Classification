The Cambodian veneration of Hanuman extends beyond simply retelling the Ramayana. His characteristics of strength, loyalty, and perseverance likely resonated deeply with the Khmer people, making him an inspirational figure. The Robam Sovann Maccha, a dance performance re-enacting an episode from the Reamker, highlights Hanuman's playful and powerful nature, entertaining audiences while reinforcing his heroic qualities.  Furthermore, his symbolic association with overcoming adversity likely contributed to his popularity and enduring presence in Cambodian culture.
